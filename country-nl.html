The Netherlands (nl)

Biometric passport: Yes

According to Wikipedia, citizens of The Netherlands can/must have biometric passports [doesn't imply retaining biometric information].

National ID card: Compulsory

According to wikipedia, The Netherlands has a compulsory identity card.

National Biometric database: Rejected

Contributed by Nir Hirshman

  • EFF:

    The Netherlands' storage of a biometric database was suspended in 2011, following privacy concerns as well as questions over the reliability of biometric technology.
